## Design Context

### Users
Active traders monitoring AI-driven positions during live market sessions. They scan screens for actionable signals — entries, exits, risk levels — and need to absorb portfolio state in seconds. They work in varied lighting (dim offices, bright daytime) and may keep the dashboard open for hours. Tolerance for visual noise is zero; every pixel must earn its place.

### Brand Personality
Sharp, clinical, authoritative. Like a Bloomberg Terminal or a surgical instrument — the design stays out of the way. The system speaks with conviction: clear directional signals, no hedging visuals, no decorative fluff. Trust is earned through precision and density, not friendliness.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Instrument-grade — dense, data-first, monospaced where it matters, tightly gridded. Think Bloomberg Terminal meets a fighter jet HUD, not a consumer fintech app.
- **Theme**: Both light and dark modes with user toggle. Dark mode is the primary (traders in dim environments); light mode must be equally dense and precise.
- **References**: Bloomberg Terminal (information density, professional gravity), institutional trading desks.
- **Anti-references**: Generic crypto dashboards (neon on black, glassmorphism, rainbow gradients). No playfulness, no toy-like elements, no decorative animations.
- **Key differentiator**: The density and legibility — this dashboard should feel like a professional tool, not a consumer app.

### Design Principles
1. **Signal over noise**: Every visual element must convey information. Decoration is forbidden. If removing something doesn't hurt comprehension, remove it.
2. **Scan-first hierarchy**: Traders scan, they don't read. Numbers, signals, and states must be readable at a glance through size, weight, and color — never through labels alone.
3. **Clinical precision**: Alignment is pixel-perfect. Spacing is systematic. Typography is intentional. Nothing is "close enough."
4. **Professional gravity**: This looks like it costs money and controls real positions. The visual weight and restraint should convey institutional seriousness.
5. **Dual-theme integrity**: Light and dark modes are equally considered. Neither is an afterthought. Both maintain the same density and hierarchy.
