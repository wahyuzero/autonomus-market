About & Guide

How It Works

  1. System monitors 18 pairs across crypto, forex, and commodities in parallel
  2. AI analyzes technicals (RSI, MACD, Ichimoku, SMC, Market Structure) every 15 seconds
  3. Multi-TP system executes with 3 targets: TP1 (1.5x ATR), TP2 (3x ATR), TP3 (5x ATR)
  4. Smart risk management: Kelly sizing, trailing SL, pyramiding, portfolio heat cap
  5. 7 safety layers filter signals: circuit breaker, correlation, session, regime, macro, news, portfolio
  6. Self-correction triggers when losses exceed 7%, AI re-evaluates strategy automatically

Risk Management

Each pair trades with $1,000 virtual balance. Position sizing uses half-Kelly Criterion capped at 15%. Portfolio heat is monitored across all pairs with daily circuit breakers. This is a simulation system - no real money is used.

AI Model

Powered by SemutSSH AI (opus-4.6 / sonnet-4.6). Analysis includes multi-timeframe confirmation, fundamental context (Fear & Greed, funding rates, news), and Smart Money Concepts detection.

Rules

  • System runs autonomous analysis every 15 seconds
  • Maximum 3 pyramid layers per pair
  • Daily circuit breaker: halts if losses exceed 3%
  • Maximum 4 consecutive losses trigger temporary pause
  • Self-correction activated when total P&L drops below -7%
